Academic & Freelance Background

From lecturing in university to independent proofreading and business education.

University Background

I have over 20 years of experience in higher education, teaching business-related subjects across universities in Ireland and abroad.

My work includes curriculum design, student mentorship, and research supervision. I'm passionate about delivering engaging, thought-provoking lectures and supporting student success.

  • Subjects Taught: Strategy, Marketing, Ethics, Management
  • Experience: Over 2 decades in university-level teaching and research
  • Institutions: Technological University Dublin, Maynooth University, more
  • Academic Leadership: Course director roles and internal moderation panels

Freelance Work

Alongside my academic career, I have developed a vibrant freelance practice in proofreading and adult education.

I work with international students and professionals to refine written English, particularly for academic and business contexts. I also deliver business training and workshops tailored to client needs.

  • Proofreading: Academic theses, research papers, and business documents
  • Business Education: Workshops on leadership, communication, and professional writing
  • Client Focus: Clear, constructive feedback tailored to multilingual learners
  • Freelance Tools: Microsoft Office Suite, Grammarly, Google Docs
