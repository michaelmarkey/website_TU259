My Transition to Tech

After building a career in academia spanning over two decades, I'm now focused on developing my technical skills through TU Dublin's Masters Qualifier in Computing. This transition builds on my analytical skills developed during my years in research and education.


During my tenure at the University of Lille as a Senior Lecturer, I coordinated language programs and managed teaching teams, developing project management abilities that transfer well to collaborative technical environments. This experience with curriculum design and program oversight has given me a structured approach to learning new technical concepts.


My experience with research methodology and data analysis in language sciences has created natural connections with database concepts and information systems. The attention to detail required in academic proofreading and article editing has likewise proven valuable when writing and debugging code.
